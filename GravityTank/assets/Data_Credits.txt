Gravity Tank Battle — asset credits
===================================

Tank sprites, bullets, explosions, bushes, barrels, and power-up props:
  Kenney — Top-down Tanks Redux
  https://kenney.nl/assets/top-down-tanks-redux
  License: Creative Commons CC0 1.0 Universal

Terrain tiles (brick / steel / water / ice) and HUD chrome are original
drawings inspired by classic Battle City / 坦克大战 layouts.

Game twist: all shells use downward gravity (parabolic arcs).

Battle City–style sound effects:
  Generated retro 8-bit WAVs from StefanBS/battle-city-clone (MIT License)
  https://github.com/StefanBS/battle-city-clone
  Classic shoot / brick / explode / power-up / stage / game-over set.
  These are open recreations in the NES tank-battle style, not Namco ROM rips.
