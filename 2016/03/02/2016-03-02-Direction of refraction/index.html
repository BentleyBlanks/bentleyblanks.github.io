Direction of refraction

已知平面法向量N⃗\vec{N}N, 入射向量L⃗\vec{L}L, 代求向量T⃗\vec{T}T, 入射夹角θ1\theta_1θ1​, 折射角θ2\theta_2θ2​，现求T⃗\vec{T}T. N⃗\vec{N}N, L⃗\vec{L}L与T⃗\vec{T}T均为方向向量.

将L⃗\vec{L}L与T⃗\vec{T}T分解到NNN和切平面上，分量记为l1,l2,t1,t2l_1, l_2, t_1, t_2l1​,l2​,t1​,t2​.

L=l1+l2L = l_1 + l_2L=l1​+l2​

l2=−N.cosθ1l2 = -N \ldotp cos\theta_1l2=−N.cosθ1​

可得

l1=L−l2=L+N.cosθ1l_1 = L - l_2 = L + N \ldotp cos\theta_1l1​=L−l2​=L+N.cosθ1​

这里t1t_1t1​与l1l_1l1​方向一致, 令sinθ1sinθ2=η=n2n1\frac{sin\theta_1}{sin\theta_2} = \eta = \frac{n_2}{n_1}sinθ2​sinθ1​​=η=n1​n2​​(菲涅尔公式)

t1=l1∗sinθ2sinθ1=l1∗1ηt_1 = l_1 \frac{sin\theta_2}{sin\theta_1} = l_1 \frac{1}{\eta}t1​=l1​∗sinθ1​sinθ2​​=l1​∗η1​

同理推出

T=t1+t2T = t_1 + t_2T=t1​+t2​

t2=−N.cosθ2t2 = -N \ldotp cos\theta_2t2=−N.cosθ2​

可得

T=l1∗1η−N∗cosθ2=L−l2=1η(L+N.cosθ1)−N∗cosθ2=1ηL+N.(1ηcosθ1−cosθ2)\begin{array}{l}
T & = & l_1 \frac{1}{\eta} - N cos\theta_2 \\
& = & L - l_2 \\
& = & \frac{1}{\eta}(L + N \ldotp cos\theta_1) - N * cos\theta_2 \\
& = & \frac{1}{\eta}L + N \ldotp (\frac{1}{\eta}cos\theta_1 - cos\theta_2)
\end{array}
