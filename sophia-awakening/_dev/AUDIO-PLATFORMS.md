# Game Audio Platforms and AI Tools

## AI / Generative Platforms

- ElevenLabs Sound Effects: text-to-SFX and ambient audio; paid plan commercial use is the safer route.
- Stable Audio / Stability AI: AI music/audio generation, available through StableAudio.com and APIs.
- AIVA: AI composition; Pro plan is positioned for full copyright ownership/commercial use.
- Beatoven.ai: royalty-free background music, explicitly lists games as a use case.
- SOUNDRAW: royalty-free AI music, WAV/stems, in-house source catalog positioning.
- Mubert: prompt/parameter-based royalty-free background music generation.
- Suno: strong song generation; free plan is not for commercial use, paid plans grant commercial use for new songs.
- Udio: strong song generation, but rights and platform terms should be checked per release.

## Asset Libraries / Marketplaces

- Kenney: CC0 game audio packs, very safe for prototypes and commercial games.
- OpenGameArt: many CC0/CC-BY/OGA-licensed assets; check each asset page.
- itch.io game assets: good for packs, but licenses vary by creator and may require purchase/attribution.
- Mixkit: free SFX/music under Mixkit license; useful but not CC0, so keep license notes.
- Freesound: excellent SFX search, but licenses vary per file.
- Pixabay Music/SFX: convenient free media license, not CC0; keep source/license notes.
